Travel to Korea












Flying to South Korea

Asiana Airlines (www.flyasiana.com) and Korean Air (www.koreanair.com) operate flights to South Korea from across the globe, including direct services from the UK and the USA.

Major airports are : Seoul Incheon International Airport and Busan Gimhae International Airport.




Plans to Travel in Korea!

Jeju Island and Busan city is the must-travel spots for foreigners to visit. In Jeju island, you can experience beuty of Korea, and in Busan city, you can enjoy Haeundae beach as well as Busan International Film Festival hold in May.

Public Transport
Bus : Passengers can choose between numerous limousines or regular inter-city buses.
The inter-city buses stop at the major districts and hotels around Seoul. Local city buses 111 and 303 link the airport to central Incheon. For a full listing of bus routes, visit the airport's website. Journey times are fares vary accroding to comfort, standard and route.
Taxi : Regular, deluxe and 'jumbo' taxis (for up to 9 people) can be hired at the airport (journey time to central Seoul : 50 min; fare from 55,000 won - 110,000won depending on taxi classification). Taxi stands are situated at the exit in Arrivals.
